Become a Medellian

We are a group of scientists, engineers, designers, and tinkerers, who have developed a group of sensors that continuously and non-invasively monitor a variety of bio-markers so that people can better manage their health.

We are a hard-working team who shares the vision of a world with preventative medicine. Our deep background in nanotechnology, micro-electronics, bio-chemistry, health informatics and software development, has allowed us to create technology that can change the way we monitor our health. If you can see eye to eye with our vision, and would like to bring your talents to work with us, we'd love to get to know you better.

Web Developer

Position Description

Medella is looking for a web application developer to create web-app for its medical devices product lines.

Responsibilities

  1. Develop web applications for various medical devices
  2. Provide front-end coding expertise across platforms and projects
  3. Implement HTML/CSS programming for relevant product and websites
  4. Research and implement website standardization and optimization
  5. Harvest feature requirements and UX design decisions
  6. Optimize front-end performance; improve user experience
  7. Complete some design related work
  8. Collaborate with team for design decisions, code development, and code review processes
  9. Assist backend developer on APIs and DBs

Requirements

  1. Bachelors degree or equivalent in computer sciences, software engineering, or related fields
  2. More than 1 year of experience building complex web applications
  3. Expertise with some of front end MVC frameworks, asset build pipelines, client and server architecture,JSON Restful Api, websockets
  4. Knowledge of Bootstrap, Vue.js, Rust, Node.js
  5. Solid expertise with HTML5/CSS3
  6. Strong knowledge of user interface design processes and methodologies
  7. Experience with Postgresql, Cloud services
  8. High motivation in learning new front-end technology/knowledge is preferred.
  9. Aptitudes in analytics, problem solving and design are preferred.
