Web Developer

Position Description

Medella is looking for a web application developer to create web-app for its medical devices product lines.

Responsibilities

  1. Develop web applications for various medical devices
  2. Provide front-end coding expertise across platforms and projects
  3. Implement HTML/CSS programming for relevant product and websites
  4. Research and implement website standardization and optimization
  5. Harvest feature requirements and UX design decisions
  6. Optimize front-end performance; improve user experience
  7. Complete some design related work
  8. Collaborate with team for design decisions, code development, and code review processes
  9. Assist backend developer on APIs and DBs

Requirements

  1. Bachelors degree or equivalent in computer sciences, software engineering, or related fields
  2. More than 1 year of experience building complex web applications
  3. Expertise with some of front end MVC frameworks, asset build pipelines, client and server architecture,JSON Restful Api, websockets
  4. Knowledge of Bootstrap, Vue.js, Rust, Node.js
  5. Solid expertise with HTML5/CSS3
  6. Strong knowledge of user interface design processes and methodologies
  7. Experience with Postgresql, Cloud services
  8. High motivation in learning new front-end technology/knowledge is preferred.
  9. Aptitudes in analytics, problem solving and design are preferred.

Hardware/Embedded Engineer

Position Description

Medella Health is looking for a hardware/embedded engineer for its wearable medical device product lines.

Responsibilities

  1. Product hardware system level design, implementation, and documentation (design specifications, component selections, schematics, PCB layouts, etc.)
  2. Complete hardware implementation and plan diagnosis tests; analyze test results and provide technical solutions for related issues.
  3. Complete embedded program design, coding, debugging and diagnosis for the product, and draft related documents.
  4. Work with process engineer and back-end developer to complete integration of prototypes; participate in industrial design and fabrication process design; provide technical support for product fabrication and tuning.
  5. Track technology development, and provide reasonable proposals for new product lines.
  6. Other daily operational duties.

Requirements

  1. Solid foundation of analog and digital circuits; strong circuit analytic and problem solving skills.
  2. Expertise in data collection circuits; familiar with various amplifiers and ADCs.
  3. Familiar with at least one hardware platform (Microcontroller/ARM/FPGA), with practical product development experience; familiar with conventional protocol (USB, Bluetooth, wifi, etc.).
  4. Expertise with EDA tools (protel/PADS/Cadence), with experience designing more than four layers of PCB.
  5. Expertise with C programming language and SoC programming environment (Keil/IAR); able to write drivers and test protocols for conventional auxiliary devices; experience with firmware development.
  6. Proficient communication skills and team work attitude.
  7. Familiarity with one of STM32, MPS430 microcontroller is preferred.
  8. More than one year of medical device embedded product-developing experience is preferred.
  9. Familiarity with EMC testing protocol and design principles is preferred.
  10. Knowledge of medical devices standards is preferred.
  11. Experience with low-energy product design is preferred.

Quality Control Engineer

Position Description

Medella Health is looking for experienced quality engineer for its in-vitro diagnosis and wearable medical devices products manufacture.

Responsibilities

  1. Design quality assurance system (QAS) for Medella’s medical device product lines.
  2. Documentation and implementation of Medella’s medical device QAS.
  3. Design and implement product verification process.
  4. Design of product fabrication/assembly environment and processes.
  5. Evaluation and selection of product component suppliers.
  6. Perform due diligence on key component suppliers.
  7. Design, implement and audit product safety evaluation procedures.
  8. Organize investigation and evaluation of quality incidents; recovery and remedy of non-qualified products.
  9. Statistical analysis of production and cost of manufacture.

Requirements

  1. Bachelor degree or equivalent; biotechnology engineering, nanotechnology engineering, electrical engineering, systems engineering background are preferred.
  2. At least two years of experience in medical devices manufacture related positions;
  3. Familiar with manufacture process of in-vitro diagnostics devices and wearable medical devices;
  4. Management experience with production, cost, quality control and equipment automation.
  5. Familiar with national/international quality control standards.
  6. Familiar with international medical device regulatory standards; experience with new (as of 2015) GMP and ISO 13485 is preferred.
  7. Aptitudes in leadership, communication, analytics and problem solving are preferred.
